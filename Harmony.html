Governance

Governance is designed to give a voice to the Harmony community holders and enable them to shape the future of the blockchain. Every Harmony community holder can choose to participate in governance directly on the harmony talk forum and Snapshot site.

Charter Ratification Process

To pass the HCIP (Harmony Improvement Proposal), proposals must receive a simple majority yes vote. All major funding proposals must go through the HCIP process in order for the initiative to be put into practice.

Charter Amendment Process
